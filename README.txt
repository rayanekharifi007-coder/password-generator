  MESSAGE FROM RAYANE(CREATOR):

This project lets you create strong, secure passwords and secretly hide messages inside normal text. If you want to chat privately with your friend, they should use the tool too so they can decrypt and read your hidden message. That way, no one else will know what you’re talking about.

It’s simple to use, lightweight, and adds an extra layer of privacy to your conversations. You protect your accounts with strong passwords and keep your chats more secure and confidential.
